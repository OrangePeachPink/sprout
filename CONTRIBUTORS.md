# Contributors

Sprout is open-source under the [MIT License](LICENSE), and its copyright line reads
*"Veronica K. Hogue and Sprout contributors."* The moment you land a PR, that
**"and contributors" is you** — and your name belongs here.

## Maintainer & initial developer

- **Veronica K. Hogue** ([@OrangePeachPink](https://github.com/OrangePeachPink)) — lead
  maintainer and initial developer: firmware, host tooling and analytics, the design system,
  and the engineering process.

## Adding your name

Everyone who lands a change is credited here, added as their first PR merges. Looking for a
place to start? **[docs/CONTRIBUTORS_WELCOME.md](docs/CONTRIBUTORS_WELCOME.md)** is our running
list of things we'd love a hand with, and **[.github/CONTRIBUTING.md](.github/CONTRIBUTING.md)**
has the work loop.
