Hi — I'm Sprout.

I look after four plants and tell you how each one's really doing — in my own words. When I'm thriving, I'll say so. When I can't feel my sensor, I'll say that too.

Flash me onto an ESP32 See how I work

Every plant, its own mood.

I read the soil in four pots and give each one a feeling, not just a figure — thriving, content, thirsty. Watch me shift: teal when I've just had a drink, amber when I'm ready for the next one.

How do I know what I'm feeling?

The same soil can read differently depending on where the probe sits — so I've learned to trust the position, not the label. Want to see how I check myself? I wrote it down.
