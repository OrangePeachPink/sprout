THE PROBLEM

Plants don't tell you
when they're thirsty.

By the time a leaf droops, the soil dried out days ago. Sprout watches the soil itself — and acts before the plant ever notices.

WHAT IT IS

four pumps,
one calm brain.

Senses moisture in each zone, continuously.
Waters automatically when a zone runs dry.
Predicts the next cycle so nothing surprises you.
